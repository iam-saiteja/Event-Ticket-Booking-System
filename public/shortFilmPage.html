National-Level Short Film Contest Rules & Regulations

Organized by Chalana Chitram BVRIT

1. Eligibility

  • The competition is open to all College students across India.
  • Participants may register individually or as a team.

2. Film Guidelines

  • The short film must be original and must not infringe on any copyrights.
  • No specific theme is required‚Äîparticipants are free to explore any genre and subject matter within the given guidelines.
  • Duration: Minimum 3 minutes, Maximum 20 minutes (including credits).
  • The film may be in any language, but English subtitles are mandatory.
  • All genres are allowed except adult content, explicit violence, or politically sensitive themes.
  • The use of AI-generated content is strictly prohibited, including AI-generated scripts, visuals, and voiceovers.
  • Licensed stock music may be used, provided proper attribution is given if required.
  • Minimum resolution: 1080p (Full HD) with a 16:9 aspect ratio.

3. Submission Guidelines

  • Films must be submitted in MP4 or MOV format.
  • Upload the film to Google Drive or YouTube (as an Unlisted video) and share the link in the registration form.
  • Submission deadline: April 9, 2025. Late submissions will not be accepted.

4. Judging Criteria

  • Films will be evaluated based on the following criteria:
  • Story & Screenplay
  • Direction
  • Acting
  • Cinematography
  • Editing

5. Screening & Selection

  • The top 10 films will be officially selected and screened at Chitrotsav 2.0 on April 11, 2025, at BV Raju Institute of Technology (BVRIT) Auditorium.
  • Shortlisted participants will be informed prior to the screenings.
  • The selected films will compete for various awards.

6. Campus Entry Rules

  • To ensure smooth event operations and effective crowd management, entry with this pass is limited to two members per team.
  • The two individuals must be from the registered Crew & Cast, including roles such as Director, Story Writer, Lead Actor, Cinematographer, or Editor.
  • Any additional team members beyond these two can join by paying an entry fee of ‚Çπ100 per person on the day of the visit.

7. General Rules

  • The organizing team reserves the right to disqualify any film that violates contest rules.
  • Plagiarism or the use of unlicensed content will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Films containing offensive, defamatory, or politically sensitive content will not be considered.
  • By submitting their films, participants grant the organizers the right to showcase their films and use clips for promotional purposes.
