Walk the Ramp as Your Favorite Star!

Step into the spotlight and bring your favorite characters to life in our exciting Cosplay Ramp Walk Contest!

Wear Your Costume, Even If You're Not Walking the Ramp!

The contest is not just about the ramp walk! You can still embrace the fun by wearing your costume and roaming around campus. Whether you participate in the contest or not, feel free to showcase your creativity and enjoy the event.

Show off your outfit, take pictures with friends, and make unforgettable memories. The more people dress up, the more fun it gets!

What is Cosplay?

Cosplay is all about creativity and passion for characters. It's your chance to dress up, perform, and embody your favorite movie or anime characters. Whether you love superheroes, iconic film stars, anime warriors, or legendary figures from cinematic history, this is the perfect event for you!

What Happens at the Cosplay Ramp Walk?

  • Participants will walk the stage like a true star, just like a fashion show, but in their amazing costumes.
  • A panel of judges will evaluate the cosplayers based on their creativity, resemblance to the character, confidence, and stage presence.
  • The best cosplayers will be awarded exciting prizes!

Who Can Participate?

Any student who loves dressing up and acting like their favorite character.

You can choose a look from:

Tollywood Bollywood Hollywood Anime Video Games Comic Books TV Shows

There are no restrictions—whether it's a superhero, villain, or legendary movie star, everyone is welcome!

How to Participate?

Get ready to own the stage and impress the audience!

Registration Rules for Cosplay Contest

Registration is only for participants who will be walking on the stage for the cosplay contest. If you are not participating in the ramp walk, you do not need to register.

However, you are still welcome to wear your costume and join the fun around the event. Enjoy the experience, take pictures, and celebrate creativity!

Ready to Showcase Your Cosplay Skills?

This is not just a contest; it's a chance to experience the thrill of performing, meet fellow cosplay enthusiasts, and create memorable moments.

So start preparing your outfits, channel your favorite characters, and get ready to make heads turn at the Cosplay Ramp Walk Contest!
