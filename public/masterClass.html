Vivek Athreya

Visionary Filmmaker & Storyteller

Vivek Athreya Garu is a visionary filmmaker known for his unique storytelling approach and character-driven narratives. His films have garnered critical acclaim and commercial success in Telugu cinema, establishing him as one of the most promising directors in contemporary Indian cinema.

With a keen eye for detail and a natural ability to blend humor with emotional depth, Vivek creates stories that resonate with audiences while pushing creative boundaries.

Notable Filmography

Ante Sundaraniki (2022) Brochevarevarura (2019) Mental Madhilo (2017) Saripodhaa Sanivaaram (2024)
